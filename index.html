Hi there. I'm Mel Perry.

I'm a User Experience and User Interface Designer with a background in marketing and B2B ecommerce. While working in marketing, I really did it all -- content development, campaign management, video creation, digital marketing -- but knew I was meant to do something else. I wanted to start a career where I could marry my love for technology, problem solving, and creativity.

Getting to this point required a signficant amount of soul searching, but in the end, I learned I really wanted to design and somehow help people.

I earned a bachelor degree of science in advertising from the wonderful West Virginia University. I have also completed the 10-week Front End Web Development course through General Assembly as well as the 6-month UX/UI Bootcamp through Georgia Tech.

Whenever I'm free, I enjoy digging through junk at thrift shop and yard sales, morning coffees with close friends, and outdoor festivals.
